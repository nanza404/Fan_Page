Rammstein Fan Page

Short bio:

Rammstein is a german heavy metal band made up of mostly east german musicians. The members of the band have remained the same since the band's inception about 20 years ago. This is an awfully long time for a successful band to stay together. They are known for their cross cultural popularity, pyrotechnic-heavy live shows, and for stirring controversy.
